# Product Recall Check

Search CPSC consumer product recalls by product name or browse by category. 9,800+ recalls since 1973 covering toys, electronics, furniture, appliances, and more.

**Live at:** https://greattombproductions.github.io/product-recalls/

## Data Source

[CPSC SaferProducts.gov Recall API](https://www.saferproducts.gov/) — The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission publishes recall data for consumer products that present a risk of injury or death.

## Features

- **Search by Product:** Type a product name (crib, space heater, hoverboard) and find all matching recalls with hazard details, injury reports, and remedy information
- **Browse by Category:** 14 product categories from Children & Toys to Fuel & Heating
- **Full Details:** Click any recall to see the complete description, hazard information, remedy instructions, units affected, and photos

## Important Note

This tool shows recalls as published by the CPSC. A recall means the product has been determined to present a risk — check if your specific product is affected using the recall details and CPSC.gov links.
